About Us

Find Foundation, Inc. is not-for-profit organization. It serves as emergency responder whose personnel carry out the necessary provision of connecting individuals in crisis situation to hospital. It also helps in providing transport, care, and first aid to patients.

Our Mission

To provide help to people and communities at a time they need information about hospitals and connect them in order to make it easier for them to access the necessary healthcare.

To also ensure safety of patients during transport by providing the fundamental healthcare while in transport.
